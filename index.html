Personal Exercise chart
Every individual is different. We at Newgen ensure that each member gets an exercise plan in accordance with their fitness level, their goal and sometimes also their preference. This tailored plan is then again updated and upgraded as the member reaches a higher fitness level with continuous guidance, support and motivation. Every body shape is different and that's why we design and recomend different exercise chart for each other.

However, it is clear that nutrition has a huge impact on the progress that you make in the gym.In addition, it will also serve as a resource and highlight the nutrient rich foods that one should incorporate in their daily gym diet plan.
